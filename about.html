Founder & CEO
Our Founder

Franco Cawagas

Founder & CEO, CocoStudio

With nearly a decade of hands-on experience in sales and marketing, I've cultivated a deep understanding of how to connect ideas with impact. My academic foundation in International Management of Entrepreneurship from St. Paul University, Quezon City, has equipped me with both the strategic mindset and global perspective needed to thrive across industries.

Driven by creativity, innovation, and a passion for delivering meaningful experiences, I founded CocoStudio to provide a seamless, full-spectrum solution for events and creative projects. By uniting a diverse range of services under one brand, we streamline execution and elevate results making it easier for clients to bring their visions to life with confidence.

My commitment to CocoStudio's mission is unwavering. Every project is a reflection of our dedication to quality, collaboration, and client satisfaction and I'm proud to lead a team that brings that promise to life with every endeavor.
